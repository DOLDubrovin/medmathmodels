The Mission of this websit

The mission of this website is:

  • to provide useful clinical tools for practical cardiology and electrophysiology, including validated cardiovascular risk scores, reference materials, and information on pacemaker and ICD programming according to current clinical knowledge and guidelines;
  • to draw attention to selected problems and perspectives in computational medicine, particularly in cardiology, electrophysiology, and physiology, and to present scientific concepts, models, and ideas developed by the author and supporting colleagues in these fields.

The initiator of the project is Oleg Dubrovin — a practicing physician, certified cardiothoracic surgeon in Russia, current resident in cardiology at the University Hospital Jena (Germany), and mathematics student at the FernUniversität in Hagen (Germany).

The development and scientific direction of the project are supported by colleagues and advisors from the fields of cardiology, electrophysiology, physiology, and related disciplines.
