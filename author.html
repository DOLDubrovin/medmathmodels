About the author

The initiator of the project, Oleg Dubrovin, graduated from the Chelyabinsk State Medical Academy in 2011. He subsequently completed postgraduate training and certification in cardiovascular surgery in Russia.

In 2022, he relocated to Germany, where he continued his postgraduate medical training in cardiology, initially at the Johanniter Hospital in Stendal (Saxony-Anhalt), and since 2024 at the University Hospital Jena.

Since April 2026, he has also been pursuing a second academic degree in the Bachelors program in Mathematics at the FernUniversität in Hagen, Germany.
